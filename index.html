Core Rekcah rules

Standard 52-card deck plus two jokers. Aces through 9 score face value, 10/J/Q/K score 10, jokers score -1. Players are eliminated at 100 cumulative points; last survivor wins.

Show pressure

A player may show only at 15 or less. A successful show scores -5 for the caller. A failed show scores a 15-point penalty, and ties always punish the caller.

Discard engine

The first player starts from six cards and may show or discard. After that, the next player may take the last discard or draw from stock, then must discard one legal group.

The simulation pits aggressive, balanced, conservative, threshold-aware, and probabilistic agents against one another, tracks show outcomes, flags meta-game patterns, and evolves strategy parameters over simulated time.
